Below is the data dictionary provided for the dataset. This dictionary will help me to get a better understanding of what the columns mean to the datase.

Product - The model no. of the treadmill¶
Age - Age of the customer in no of years¶
Gender - Gender of the customer¶
Education - Education of the customer in no. of years¶
Marital Status - Marital status of the customer¶
Usage - Avg. # times the customer wants to use the treadmill every week¶
Fitness - Self rated fitness score of the customer (5 - very fit, 1 - very unfit)¶
Income - Income of the customer¶
Miles- Miles that a customer expects to run¶

Customer profile¶

  • Observations = The were 180 observations noted in the dataset. I believe these are representing the range of customers.
  • Average age of the customers is 28 years with the youngest person person being 18years old and oldest person being 50 years old.
  • Education: - About 50% of the customers have 16 years of education out of the maximum number of 21 years. Looking the spread of educutions, I believe it's safe to say that the customers for this fitness products have at least high school education or 12 years.
  • Income- The average customer for these products makes a little over 53,719USD in income, with most income being 104,581USD
  • Fitness - The average person is kind of fit with a score of 3 on a 1-5 gradient. 1 being unfit and 5 being very fit. 75% of the customers recorded 4 with 25% of the customers recorded 3.
  • Usage: - 50% of the customers will love to use the treadmill 3 times a week while 75% will love to use it 4 times a week.
